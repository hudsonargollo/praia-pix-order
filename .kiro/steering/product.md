# Product Overview

Coco Loko Açaiteria is a complete digital ordering and payment system for an açaí shop by the beach. The system provides a seamless experience for customers to order via QR codes at tables, make payments through PIX, and receive WhatsApp notifications.

## Key Features

- **Customer Flow**: QR code menu access, digital ordering, PIX payments, WhatsApp notifications
- **Kitchen Dashboard**: Real-time view of paid orders, order status management
- **Cashier Panel**: Order monitoring and customer notification system
- **Role-based Access**: Protected routes for kitchen and cashier staff

## Target Users

- **Customers**: Beach-goers ordering açaí and refreshments
- **Kitchen Staff**: Food preparation team managing order queue
- **Cashier Staff**: Front-of-house team handling customer service and notifications

The application focuses on streamlining operations for a small açaí shop while providing a modern, mobile-first customer experience.
